The Shelter's Dilemma

Shelters are the frontline of animal rescue, but they face two critical barriers that limit their impact.

1. The Resource Barrier

Shelters and non-profits are overwhelmed and under-resourced. Their current process for creating pet profiles is slow and manual.

  • ! Overwhelmed Staff: Every minute spent on data entry is a minute not spent on animal care.
  • ! Low Engagement: Manual, hasty posts struggle to reach a wide audience or capture an adopter's heart.

2. The "Cloud AI" Barrier

Powerful generative AI seems like the obvious solution, but for a non-profit, it may be expensive and not affordable.

  • $ Too Expensive: Server costs and per-API-call fees for every pet profile are unsustainable for a donation-funded shelter.
  • ? Too Complex: Requires technical hurdles and raises valid data privacy concerns for shelter operations.

Shelters have been locked out of the AI revolution. Until now.

Our Solution: AI for *Everyone*

We use Chrome's built-in AI to give shelters a 'superpower.' All features run instantly, privately, and for free, directly on-device. Explore the features below to see how it works.
